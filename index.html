ABOUT US


Mechanical Engineering Society of BIT Sindri was established to meet the diversified challenges and bring out innovative approach in the fields of Mechanical engineering. It provides industrial exposure to draw the practical application of concepts and avails its members a platform to enrich their management skills. Under the guidance of HOD Dr. S.K. Singh sir and other associate professors, Mechanical Engineering Society is progressing towards achieving its goals. Mechanical Engineering Society B.I.T Sindri is a technical society in B.I.T Sindri which focuses on the wholesome development of the students. Mechanical Engineering Society B.I.T Sindri conducts many events, seminars, guest lectures, workshops throughout the year and last but not the least MES organizes the Annual techfest “YANTRIKA” where budding engineers from different colleges participate to prove their might. With the motto of holistic development, Mechanical Engineering Society focuses on the personality devlopment and enhancement of technical skills of students in B.I.T Sindri.
